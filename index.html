About Grove

Grove was started in 2019 by two coffee obsessives who grew tired of drinking beans that had crossed two continents and sat in a warehouse for six months. When we couldn't find what we wanted, we went to the source ourselves.

Today we work directly with seven farms across three countries. Every lot is tasted, approved, and roasted within weeks of arrival. No middlemen. No mystery. Just coffee that tastes like somewhere real.

How it works

From farm to your front door.

01

Source

We visit origin farms every harvest season, cup samples on-site, and buy directly. We know the farmers by name.

02

Roast

Every batch is roasted fresh at our facility on a 12 kg drum roaster. We pull at peak development — no longer.

03

Ship

Roasted-to-order bags are sealed, labelled with roast date, and dispatched within 48 hours. Freshness guaranteed.
